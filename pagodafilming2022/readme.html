Vietnam 2022 - Cinematography and Filming

A collection of videos taken throughout the Exploring Employability and South East Asian Culture in Vietnam DMU Global experience. The trip took place in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam, and is in partnership and assosociation with Pagoda Projects and De Montfort University.
